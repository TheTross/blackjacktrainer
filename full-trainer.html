How to Use This Trainer

Treat this like a flash‑card machine for decisions. Aim for slow, accurate answers rather than speed, then gradually reduce your reliance on any printed strategy chart.[web:270][web:272]

  • Start on “Mixed” to get a general sense of your accuracy, then drill your weakest category.
  • When you miss a hand, pause and think about why the correct action makes sense before moving on.
  • Pair this trainer with the Probability and Mistakes lessons to connect the logic behind each play.

The goal is to make correct decisions feel automatic so you have more bandwidth for table awareness and responsible limits.

Important Notes

  • This trainer uses one fixed rule set and a built‑in strategy table; real casinos may have different rules.[web:271]
  • It ignores side bets and card counting and focuses only on basic strategy decisions.[web:266][web:270]
  • All practice here is for educational purposes only and does not guarantee any outcome in real play.[web:274]
