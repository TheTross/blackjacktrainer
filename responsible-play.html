What Responsible Play Means

Responsible play assumes that gambling is optional entertainment, not a financial strategy. It focuses on staying within pre‚Äëset limits and being willing to stop when those limits are reached.

  • Clear boundaries: deciding in advance how much time and money you are comfortable spending.
  • Separate funds: using only money that is not needed for bills, savings, or obligations.
  • Awareness of warning signs: chasing losses, hiding play, or gambling when stressed.
  • Willingness to pause or stop: viewing breaks, self‚Äëexclusion, or limits as healthy tools, not failures.
